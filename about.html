Meet the Doctor

Dr First graduated from the University of Missouri-St. Louis and Southern Illinois University School of Dental Medicine. She also has a residency from SIU in implant dentistry. While in dental school, Dr. First received numerous awards, including the Removable Prosthodontics Award, Pierre Fauchard Academy Award and was inducted into the National Dental Honor Society.

A past President of the Greater St. Louis Dental Society, Dr. First sits on the American Dental Association's Committee on Dental Education and Licensure. She is also a Clinical Associate Professor at the Southern Illinois University School of Dental Medicine. Dr. First is a fellow of the American College of Dentists and the International College of Dentists. She is on the Peer Review Committee for the Greater St. Louis Dental Society as well as a delegate from St. Louis to the Missouri House of Delegates. Dr. First also sees patients from Donated Dental Services of Missouri.

In her spare time, she enjoys sailing, golfing and playing with her granddaughter.

Personal Relationships

Our favorite thing about the work we do is the close-knit bond we build with our patients. We truly love the opportunity we have to serve you and your family through the years and to be a part of improving your health and your life. We love getting to know you, and we enjoy hearing about everything going on in your life! You'll feel like family here because to us, you are!


Passion for Dentistry

Why is Dr. First a dentist? Because she loves dentistry! Her passion is evident in the high-quality care she delivers and the kind, personal way she interacts with her patients. She is always striving for excellence and seeks daily to better herself so she can better treat you. You won't find another dentist around that's more dedicated and more passionate about improving your smile than Dr. First.


Dental Excellence

Dr. First began working in the dental field at this practice when she was 19 years old. Since then, she has received the skills, training and experience to deliver the highest standard of excellence. Not only does she deliver every service with careful expertise and superior results, she is also kind, caring and dedicated to always doing what's best for you. She aims for excellence and proudly delivers.


Comfortable Environment

We want you to be comfortable with the treatment you receive and be comfortable while receiving treatment. Our compassionate team strives to create a clean and calming environment at our practice to help you feel at ease. We are friendly, helpful and fun! We work hard to make your visit with us one of the best around.


On-Time Appointments

We understand that your life is busy and your time is valuable. Because we respect you and value your time, we make every possible effort to maintain on-time appointments. We promise to never double book or leave you unattended in the waiting room. We are ready and available to assist with any need.


Lasting Results

Did your insurance provider or previous dentist recommend a quick, cheap fix for your care that didn't solve the actual problem? We don't do that here! Our number one goal in every treatment is to deliver customized care with results that last for the rest of your life! We take our time when diagnosing your dental condition, and we will carefully review all your treatment options until we find the best solution to meet your individual needs. We deliver lasting results we proudly stand behind.

If you value these qualities in a dentist, call us today! We’re passionate about your smile and passionate about helping you achieve optimal health!
