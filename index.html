Dental Implants

Dental implants are designed to provide a secure foundation for replacement teeth that look, feel and function like natural teeth. If you are among the many Americans who suffer from permanent tooth loss, you can eliminate the problems and discomfort caused by poorly-fitting dentures or missing teeth. You can

begin to discover the joy of eating healthy, speaking clearly, feeling confident and laughing comfortably! With dental implants, you can rediscover the comfort and confidence to enjoy your life. Dental implants are designed to provide you with a natural-looking and functioning smile.

Dental Services

We are a full service family, general & cosmetic dental office. The list below is a sample of a few of the services we provide.

Family Dentistry

Dr Louise R First is thrilled to provide dental services to patients of all ages. We invite you to bring your whole family to our dental office located in St Louis, MO.

Restorative Dentistry

If you have cavities, lost or loose teeth, and tooth pain that disrupts your daily schedule, our dental office will provide the advanced restorative dentistry to improve your smile and your life.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Louise First is an experienced cosmetic dentist who understands how to customize each procedure exactly to fit your needs.

Preventative Dentistry

We look to prevent oral problems through regular dental visits, oral health education, and proper oral hygiene. This means you will be able to maintain optimum oral health and avoiding costly oral health issues.
