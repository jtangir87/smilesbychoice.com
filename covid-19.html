COVID-19 Policies

  1. Home
  2. COVID-19 Policies

At the office of Dr. Louise First, the health and well-being of our patients and our employees is our top priority. We understand your concern and uncertainty surrounding the Corona Virus and we are committed to being responsive to your needs as the situation evolves.

We would like to reassure you that we already utilize standard disease control precautions as outlined by the CDC and are currently exceeding these precautions to help prevent the spread of this virus and maintain a safe environment. Our office is open for all your dental care. We will be listening to the recommendations of local and state health authorities and will advise you of any changes to our schedule. When you arrive for a scheduled appointment, call or text us from your car and wait until we text or call you back. To help protect the safety of our patients and staff, we ask anyone who feels ill, to reschedule. This is especially true if you have a fever, cough, sneezing or shortness of breath. If you have had these symptoms in the last 48 hours this would also apply. If you have traveled out of the US or St. Louis bistate area in the last 2 weeks we also ask that you reschedule. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to call.

Sincerely,
Louise R. First DMD and Staff
